Digital

Digital kits now follow the same gallery logic.

Digital and social assets share the same image naming convention (-thumb / -wide) and can be added as a new category in assets/js/main.js without changing layouts.

Add a category

  1. Create /images/sections/digital/<project>/ folders with thumb + wide pairs.
  2. Add a digital array to portfolioData with IDs, titles, descriptions, and image basenames.
  3. Create a page with data-category="digital" or add a filter pill on the homepage.
