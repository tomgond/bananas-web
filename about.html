About Mor Ben-Dror

Illustrator & graphic designer behind Bananas – Illustration & Branding.

Portrait Placeholder

Bananas energy, Grotesca edge

Mor Ben-Dror is an illustrator and graphic designer crafting identities, live event drawings, and digital visuals that pop in black and banana yellow. Bananas studio blends playful storytelling with disciplined systems thinking so brands feel both expressive and precise.

From quick-turn conference sketching to full identity suites (BBB Kids Club, Caktus, Summit and more), Mor builds visuals that stay bold across print, social, and web. The Grotesca-inspired redesign keeps the focus on strong shapes, generous negative space, and surprising interactions.

Based in Haifa, available worldwide. Let’s make something bright together.
